## ‚ö†Ô∏è Risks & Mitigation

### Risk 1: Low User Adoption of Gamification
**Likelihood**: Low  
**Impact**: High  
**Mitigation**:
- Extensive beta testing before full launch
- A/B test different game mechanics
- Option to disable gamification per user
- Focus on core marketplace value first

### Risk 2: Meme Culture Shifts
**Likelihood**: Medium  
**Impact**: Medium  
**Mitigation**:
- Build flexible system that works beyond specific meme
- Focus on underlying psychology (binary choice) not just meme
- Ability to pivot to new trending concepts
- Core marketplace value independent of gamification

### Risk 3: Fraud & Gaming the System
**Likelihood**: High  
**Impact**: Medium  
**Mitigation**:
- Already have fraud detection AI in place
- Rate limiting and anomaly detection
- Human review for suspicious patterns
- Penalties for abuse (ban, streak reset)

### Risk 4: Can't Compete with Giants
**Likelihood**: Medium  
**Impact**: High  
**Mitigation**:
- Target underserved niche (gamification-responsive users)
- Build community moat (hard to replicate culture)
- Move fast and iterate quickly
- Focus on categories giants ignore

### Risk 5: High CAC, Low LTV
**Likelihood**: Low  
**Impact**: High  
**Mitigation**:
- Gamification drives organic viral growth (lower CAC)
- Engagement mechanics improve retention (higher LTV)
- Already tracking unit economics closely
- Multiple revenue streams reduce dependency
